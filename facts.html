Fun Facts

  • Nutcracking - Crows drop walnuts on the road and sit back to let the cars do the work for them!

  • Talking - Crows are able to mimic some human speech very well, especially in captivity!

  • Preening - Preening strengthens the pair bond between crows and keeps their feathers in good shape.
